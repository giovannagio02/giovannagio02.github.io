    About Skødstrup

    Skødstrup Parish is a parish in Århus Nordre Provsti.
    In the 19th century was an independent parish.
    Skødstrup parish municipality was incorporated into Aarhus Municipality
    during the municipal reform in 1970.
    In the parish there are the many authorized place names
    like: Bondehaven, Løgten, Elishøj and Åstrup.
    Løgten is a station town close to forest, beach, and the big city of Aarhus.
