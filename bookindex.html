    Bøger og Ting!

    A book store managed by a retired librarian with the passion of children's books.
    A quite, cozy and vintage place,
    perfect for the passioned ones for books.
    A time jump back in the past, memories of a grandma's house,
    you can feel the love of a grandma engulfing you from the walls.
    The shop offers a wide range of Danish book, but also a variety of books in English.
